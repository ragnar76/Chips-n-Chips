   TT TOS (TOS 3.01)
   ------

   TOS 3.01 is the first version of TOS that originally shipped in the
   TT030.

        Formats:  4 chip ROMs (a 1MB Eprom DIL-Chip)

       Machines:  TT030

      Utilities:  CACHEXXX.PRG, FOLDRXXX.PRG

                  CACHEXXX.PRG und FOLDRXXX.PRG are explained above.

          Notes:  This version is only avaible in the TT030. It isn't
                  available for upgrading.



   TT TOS (TOS 3.05)
   ------

   TOS 3.05 is the second version of TOS currently shipped in the
   TT030.


        Formats:  4 chip ROMs (a 1MB Eprom DIL-Chip)

       Machines:  TT030

      Utilities:  CACHEXXX.PRG, FOLDRXXX.PRG, SERPTCH1.PRG, SERPTCH2.PRG

                  CACHEXXX.PRG und FOLDRXXX.PRG are explained above.

          Notes:  This version is only avaible in the TT030.  It isn't
                  available for upgrading.
