   CDAR504 Specifications

   Standard                Sony und Philipps CD-Rom standard Mode 1 & 2
   Data Readout from Disc  153 kilobyte per second (continious)
   Traverse Time           Less than 1.0 second (sequential move from
                           innermost to outermost track)
   Bit Error Rate          10^-12 (unscratched disc)
   Interface               ACSI (two 19-pin connectors)
   Audio Output            External: 1.4 V RMS 20 Ohm load
                           Headphones: volume control
   Frequency Response      20 to 20kHz +1/-6 decibels
   Sound Noise Ratio       -75 decibels (limit -70 decibels)
   Operating Temperatur    41 to 104 Grad F (5 to 40 Grad C)
   (noncondensing)
   Humidity                20 to 80%
   (noncondensing)
   AC Voltage              117V (60Hz)
   Power Consumption       0.15A
   Installation            Horizontal
   Loading                 Front loading
   Display                 2 Digits
   Remote Control          Audio control
   Dimensions              2.85 in. (7cm)* 13in. (33cm) * 11.4in. (29cm)
   Weight                  9.5lb. (4.3kg)
