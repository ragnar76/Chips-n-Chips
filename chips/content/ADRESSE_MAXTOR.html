                             MAXTOR Corp.,
                   headquartered in San Jose, Calif.,
       designs, manufactures and markets magnetic storage products -
                from high-performance and high-capacity
                      to low cost and light weight,
           ranging from 84 megabyte to 1.7 gigabyte capacity
      in the 1.8-inch, 2.5-inch, 3.5-inch and 5.25-inch form factors.
            Maxtor employs approximately 8,800 people worldwide.
               Its stock trades under the NASDAQ symbol MXTR.
