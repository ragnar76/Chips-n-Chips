   15 Pin VGA-Buchse (TT030) auf 9 Pin Monitor-Stecker (ECL-Video)

   Eizo 6500 und Eizo 6500M Monitoranschlußkabel

   15 Pin               9 Pin
   Pin 4  <-----------> Pin 1 (+ECL, MSB)
   Pin 5  <-----------> Pin 7+8 (Ground)
       +6+7+8+9+10
   Pin 13 <-----------> Pin 5 (HSync)
   Pin 14 <-----------> Pin 9 (VSync)
   Pin 15 <-----------> Pin 6 (-ECL, MSB)

   Unbenutzte Pins am Eizo 6500 und 6500M
                        Pin 2 (-ECL, LSB)
                        Pin 3 (+ECL, LSB)
                        Pin 4 open circuit ***


   +ECL (MSB) und -ECL (MSB) müssen 75 Ohm Videoleitung sein!

   Keinesfalls sollte man auf der Monitorseite noch +ECL und -ECL [MSB]
   jeweils noch mit ihrem [LSB]-Kollegen verbinden, das verschlechtert
   unter umständen das Bild erheblich.

   Bei EIZO Monitoren muß noch der interne H-POS-Regler für ECL verdreht
   werden, weil die Bildlagen von VGA und ECL nicht zueinander passen.


   MSB = most-significant bit
   LSB = least-significant bit
   *** = this connection is left opened, and should be internally
         selected either to -5Vdc or to -7.5Vdc depending on the
         ECL board.

   ECL used is 10K series 2-bit input with the following 4 intensity
   levels:

   MSB     LSB     Out     Intensity
   ---------------------------------
    0       0       0      black
    0       1      1/3     dark grey
    1       0      2/3     light grey
    1       1      3/3     white

   Other stuff that may be usefull (only 6500M):

        The monitor will auto-detect whether the
        H and V sync signals are separate, composite
        on pin 5, or composite on pin 9.

        Horiz Scan Frequency = 31.5 KHz and 56-80 KHz
                                (auto-detected)
        Vert Scan Frequency = 55-90 Hz
                                (auto-detected)
        Video bandwidth = 120 MHz
        Video Amplifier Rise/Fall time = 5.0 ns max
        ECL signal polarity = negative
        Max. Power Consumption = 130 Watts
        Max. Input Current = 1.2A
